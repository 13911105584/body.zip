Body.zip

[A Body Memory Project]


Body.zip is an archival project that collects snapshots of body parts contributed by our body donors. Each folder is compressed into an easily disseminable package; upon expansion, you will find folders containing neat photographic divisions of the donor's body, along with a README.TXT file that contains information on the donor's physical properties, identity markers, as well as how they would like their digital body parts to be handled online.


To contribute your body:

Take a SQUARE photograph (rear-facing or front-facing/selfie) of each of the following body parts:

  1. FACE / FOREHEAD
  2. NECK / THROAT AREA
  3. LEFT HAND, RIGHT HAND
  4. LEFT FOREARM, RIGHT FOREARM
  5. LEFT UPPER ARM, RIGHT UPPER ARM
  6. STOMACH / TORSO
  7. LEFT THIGH, RIGHT THIGH
  8. LEFT SHIN, RIGHT SHIN
  9. LEFT FOOT, RIGHT FOOT


Download the following Textedit questionnaire file and save a new copy with your responses.




Place the photos and revised Textedit file into a folder and title it FIRSTNAME_LASTNAME_MM/DD/YY. Compress the folder into a .zip file.


Send your .zip folder to xxu@risd.edu.
